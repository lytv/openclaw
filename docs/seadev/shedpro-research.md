## 1. Product Overview

### What is ShedPro?

ShedPro is a specialized digital marketing and sales platform designed specifically for the shed and portable building industry (including carports, barns, and other outdoor structures). They position themselves as an "all-in-one solution" helping shed businesses achieve **"More Sales. Less Work."**

### Core Value Proposition

- Combines 3D visualization technology with marketing automation
- Helps shed builders sell more products online
- Provides complete sales funnel: visual product exploration → Google Ads lead generation → operations management

### Key Products

| Product | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **3D Configurator** | Real-time product visualization with customization |
| **Sales-Focused Websites** | Custom branded websites with e-commerce |
| **Google Ads Management** | Certified Google Partner managing paid advertising |
| **Online Inventory System** | E-commerce product management |
| **Operations Hub** | Business management platform |

---

## 2. Target Customer

### Ideal Customer Profile

- **Shed manufacturers** - Companies that build sheds at scale
- **Shed dealers** - Retailers selling portable buildings to consumers
- **Shed builders** - Independent contractors and small businesses
- **Carport businesses** - Related portable structure sellers

### Pain Points Addressed

- Difficulty selling complex customizable products online
- High customer hesitation when ordering unseen products
- Managing leads, inventory, and operations separately
- Low average order values due to lack of upselling tools
- Ineffective Google Ads campaigns

## 8. Technology Stack

### Likely Technologies Used

| Technology | Purpose |
|------------|---------|
| **Three.js** | Core WebGL 3D rendering |
| **React Three Fiber** | React integration for Three.js |
| **React/Vue.js** | Frontend framework |
| **Node.js/Python** | Backend pricing engine |
| **PostgreSQL/MySQL** | Product database |
| **GLTF/GLB** | 3D model format (compressed) |

### Architecture Overview

```
Frontend: React + Three.js/WebGL
    ↓ REST/GraphQL
Backend: Node.js Pricing Engine
    ↓
Database: Product catalog, options, pricing rules
```

## 10. Key Findings

1. **Unique Niche:** ShedPro is a specialized B2B player in the ~$10B US shed industry
2. **Differentiator:** Proprietary 3D visualization technology + turnkey solution
3. **Business Model:** Combines SaaS tools with managed services (high-touch partnership)
4. **Market Opportunity:** Fragmented industry with many small regional manufacturers lacking digital tools
5. **Low Competition:** No direct B2B software competitor found targeting shed builders specifically
