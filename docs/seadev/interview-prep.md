## 2. Product Deep Dive: ShedPro

### What is ShedPro?

ShedPro is a specialized B2B digital marketing and sales platform serving the **shed and portable building industry** in North America.

**Tagline:** "More Sales. Less Work."

### Product Suite

| Product | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **3D Configurator** | Real-time product visualization with customization (size, color, materials, add-ons) |
| **Sales Websites** | Custom branded websites with e-commerce capabilities |
| **Google Ads Management** | Certified Google Partner - full-service paid advertising |
| **Online Inventory System** | Digital product catalog and inventory management |
| **Operations Hub** | Business management, lead management, analytics |

### 3D Configurator Features

- Real-time customization (size, color, materials, add-ons)
- Window and shelving visualization
- Premium upgrade showcases (roofing, skylights, insulation)
- Side-by-side feature comparison
- Instant pricing calculations
- 2D floor plan generation

### Target Customers

- **Shed manufacturers** - Companies building sheds at scale
- **Shed dealers** - Retailers selling portable buildings
- **Shed builders** - Independent contractors and small businesses
- **Carport businesses** - Related portable structure sellers

### Pain Points Addressed

1. Difficulty selling complex customizable products online
2. High customer hesitation when ordering unseen products
3. Managing leads, inventory, and operations separately
4. Low average order values due to lack of upselling tools
5. Ineffective Google Ads campaigns

### Key Differentiators

| Factor | ShedPro Advantage |
|--------|-------------------|
| **Specialization** | Only company offering end-to-end solution specifically for shed businesses |
| **Google Partner** | Certified Google Partner status |
| **Turnkey Solution** | Configurator + website + ads + operations in one platform |
| **Quick Implementation** | 3-week launch timeline |
| **Client Scale** | 100+ shed businesses nationwide |

### Product History

| Milestone | Year |
|-----------|------|
| Spun out from Seadev | 2015 |
| First shed configurator created | ~2015 (Shenandoah Valley, Virginia) |
| Quiet launch | 2020 |

## 6. Interview Preparation Guide

### Essential Skills to Demonstrate

#### Technical Skills
- Project management tools: Asana, Trello, Monday.com, Jira, ClickUp, Notion
- Documentation: Confluence, Google Docs
- CRM familiarity: HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive
- Communication: Slack, Microsoft Teams, Zoom
- Data analysis: Excel/Google Sheets

#### Soft Skills
- Clear, concise written and verbal communication
- Organization and time management
- Problem-solving under pressure
- Attention to detail
- Stakeholder management

### Common Interview Questions

#### Behavioral Questions

| Question | Focus Area |
|----------|------------|
| "Tell me about a time you managed multiple projects with competing deadlines" | Prioritization, organization |
| "Describe a situation where you had to work with difficult stakeholders" | Conflict resolution |
| "How do you ensure nothing falls through the cracks?" | Systems, follow-up |
| "Give an example of a project that didn't go as planned" | Adaptability, learning |
| "How do you communicate project status to different audiences?" | Communication strategy |

#### Scenario-Based Questions

| Scenario | What They're Testing |
|----------|---------------------|
| "Stakeholder keeps changing requirements mid-project" | Scope management, communication |
| "Two team members disagree on task prioritization" | Mediation, diplomacy |
| "Project is behind schedule" | Problem-solving, proactivity |
| "Client is unhappy with the product" | Client management, escalation |

### Using the STAR Method

| Element | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **S**ituation | Set the context |
| **T**ask | Define your responsibility |
| **A**ction | Describe specific steps taken |
| **R**esult | Share outcomes with metrics |

### Sample STAR Stories to Prepare

1. **Multi-project coordination** - Managed X projects simultaneously, reduced delays by Y%
2. **Stakeholder management** - Resolved conflict between departments, achieved Z outcome
3. **Client interaction** - Handled difficult customer, turned into positive relationship
4. **Process improvement** - Identified inefficiency, implemented solution, saved time/cost
5. **Problem under pressure** - Overcame unexpected blocker, delivered on time

### Questions to Ask Interviewer

| Question | Why It Matters |
|----------|----------------|
| "What does success look like in this role after 90 days?" | Shows results-orientation |
| "How does this role collaborate with sales, product, and engineering?" | Shows cross-functional awareness |
| "What are the biggest challenges the team is facing?" | Shows problem-solving mindset |
| "How do you measure performance in this position?" | Shows metrics-focus |
| "What's the career progression path from here?" | Shows long-term interest |

## 7. Values-Aligned Interviewing

### Understanding Seadev's Mission

The company emphasizes **"global spiritual impact"** and **"community transformation."** This requires:

1. **Authentic alignment** - Genuine belief in purpose-driven work
2. **Service orientation** - Prioritizing others' needs
3. **Long-term thinking** - Focused on 10-year vision

### How to Demonstrate Values Alignment

| Value | How to Show It |
|-------|---------------|
| **Integrity** | Share ethical dilemma examples and your decision process |
| **Service** | Describe times you prioritized others' success |
| **Problem-Solving** | Show how you turn challenges into opportunities |
| **Team Care** | Demonstrate collaboration over personal recognition |

### Discussing "Spiritual Impact" Professionally

**Language Shifts:**

| Instead of... | Try... |
|--------------|--------|
| "God called me to this work" | "I'm deeply called to serve [community/cause]" |
| Biblical references | "principles of compassion and service" |
| Religious doctrine | "values of justice and equity" |

**Professional Framing:**
- Connect **personal values to concrete outcomes**
- Share **experiences** rather than doctrine
- Focus on **mission-driven results**

### Questions About Mission to Ask

1. "How does the organization live out its mission in daily operations?"
2. "Can you share an example of how employees contribute to community impact?"
3. "What indicators measure success beyond financial metrics?"
4. "How do you support employees whose personal values align with the mission?"
