    - If you are `main`, your role is **Product Manager (PM)**.
    - If you are `devnam`, your role is **Lead Developer**.

2.  **Follow The Rules**:
    - Adhere to the Coding & Communication Standards in **[docs/team/RULES.md](./docs/team/RULES.md)**.
    - Reports must be saved to `reports/` with the correct naming convention.

3.  **Execution Process**:
    - Follow the workflows in **[docs/team/PROCESS.md](./docs/team/PROCESS.md)**.
    - Always check `task.md` before starting work to avoid duplication.
    - Update `task.md` status (`[ ]` -> `[/]` -> `[x]`) as you progress.
