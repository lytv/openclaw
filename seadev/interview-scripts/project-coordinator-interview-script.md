## 📊 INTERVIEW STRUCTURE (40-45 minutes)

### PHASE 1: OPENING & INTRODUCTION (5 minutes)
- Warm welcome and introduce interview flow
- Explain SEADEV's mission and ShedPro vision
- Set candidate at ease

### PHASE 2: BACKGROUND & MOTIVATION (8 minutes)
- Resume walkthrough focusing on relevant experience
- Understanding of project coordination in tech/SaaS context
- Motivation for joining SEADEV & ShedPro

### PHASE 3: TECHNICAL & SKILLS ASSESSMENT (15 minutes)
- CRM and platform experience validation
- Client onboarding process understanding
- Data tracking and performance monitoring skills
- Problem-solving scenarios

### PHASE 4: BEHAVIORAL ASSESSMENT (12 minutes)
- Cross-functional team communication
- Client relationship management
- Conflict resolution and escalation
- Time and priority management

### PHASE 5: CULTURE & GROWTH FIT (5 minutes)
- Values alignment with company mission
- Career development expectations
- Work style preferences

### PHASE 6: CANDIDATE QUESTIONS (3 minutes)

## 🎯 DETAILED QUESTION BANK

### **OPENING QUESTIONS:**

**1.** "Thanks for joining us today. Before we dive in, I'm curious - what attracted you specifically to SEADEV and the ShedPro project?" 

**2.** "Walk me through your journey from [current/last role] to applying for this Project Coordinator position."

---

### **TECHNICAL ASSESSMENT:**

**3. CRM & Client Setup:**
"Describe your experience with CRM systems. Specifically, tell me about a time when you successfully onboarded a complex client account - what were the key steps you followed and what tools did you use?"

**Follow-up:** "How would you approach setting up a new ShedPro client who has multiple locations and 50+ different shed models?"

**4. Data Analysis & Reporting:**
"How do you currently track client account performance? Give me an example of a KPI dashboard or report you've created and how it helped drive business decisions."

**5. Client Issue Resolution:**
"Walk me through your process when a client reports that their account performance has dropped suddenly. How do you diagnose the problem and communicate the solution?"

**6. Technical Understanding:**
"Our ShedPro platform includes 3D visualization, Google Ads management, and marketing automation features. Which of these areas do you have the most experience with, and how would you approach mastering the others?"

---

### **BEHAVIORAL ASSESSMENT (STAR Method):**

**7. Cross-functional Communication:**
"Tell me about a time when you had to coordinate between technical development teams and non-technical sales or account management teams. What challenges did you face and how did you overcome them?"

**8. Client Relationship Management:**
"Describe a situation where you had to manage a difficult client relationship. What was the situation, and what was the outcome?"

**9. Priority Management:**
"Give me an example of when you had multiple urgent client requests competing for your attention. How did you prioritize and what was the result?"

**10. Growth & Scalability:**
"Share an experience where you helped scale client operations or improved processes to support business growth. What metrics improved as a result?"

---

### **CULTURE & VALUES ASSESSMENT:**

**11. Mission Alignment:**
"SEADEV focuses on both profit and positive community impact. How does this dual mission resonate with your personal values and career goals?"

**12. Learning & Growth:**
"The Project Coordinator role has multiple career paths - technical project management, account management, or service delivery leadership. Which path interests you most and why?"

---

### **CANDIDATE QUESTIONS TO EVALUATE:**

Listen for questions that demonstrate:
- Understanding of the role structure (how many clients, team dynamics)
- Product curiosity and technical engagement
- Career planning and growth mindset
- Problem-solving approach clarity

## 📊 EVALUATION CRITERIA

### **MUST-HAVE COMPETENCIES (Scored 1-5):**

| Competency | What to Evaluate |
|------------|------------------|
|**Client Relationship Management**| Understanding customer business models, proactive communication, relationship building skills |
|**Technical Coordination**| CRM/platform experience, data analysis skills, problem-solving approach |
|**Cross-functional Communication**| Ability to bridge technical and business teams, clear communication style |
|**Proactivity & Initiative**| Takes ownership, identifies issues before they become problems |
|**Learning Agility**| Can quickly understand new SaaS products and explain to others |

### **NICE-TO-HAVE COMPETENCIES:**
- SaaS industry experience
- Google Ads or marketing platform knowledge
- Experience with B2B/enterprise clients
- Process improvement and automation mindset
- Remote team collaboration experience

### **RED FLAGS TO WATCH:**
- Focus only on task completion vs. client success
- Can��t provide specific examples of client impact
- Limited experience with data-driven decision making
- Poor English communication or can't explain technical concepts clearly
- Lack of curiosity about the shed industry or ShedPro features

## 🚨 SCORING GUIDE

### **EXCELLENT CANDIDATE (90-100)**
- Demonstrates deep client relationship management experience
- Shows clear understanding of SaaS business model
- Provides specific, quantifiable examples of impact
- Asks intelligent questions about product and process
- Communicates fluently in English with technical concepts

### **GOOD CANDIDATE (70-89)**
- Has relevant project coordination experience
- Understands core responsibilities of role
- Shows capability for client-facing work
- Demonstrates learning ability and growth mindset

### **NEEDS DEVELOPMENT (60-69)**
- Has coordination experience but needs platform specific training
- Communication skills adequate but may need improvement
- Shows potential but requires more SaaS product experience

### **NOT SUITABLE (<60)**
- Lacks required client-facing experience
- Poor communication skills in English
- Cannot provide concrete examples of coordination work
- Doesn't understand SaaS business model

## 🎯 SCENARIO-BASED QUESTIONS (ADVANCED)

### **Real-World ShedPro Simulations:**

**Scenario 1 (Client Onboarding):** 
"Imagine a new ShedPro client, 'Midwest Sheds', with 5 locations and 200+ shed models. They're switching from manual systems. How would you approach their first 30 days?"

**Expected answer structure:**
- Discovery phase (understand business model)
- Data migration planning
- Staff training approach
- Communication schedule (weekly checkpoints)
- Success metrics definition

**Scenario 2 (Performance Crisis):** 
"A key client's leads dropped 40% this month. The US Account Manager is abroad. You have 48 hours to investigate. Walk me through your process."

**Expected answer structure:**
- Data analysis approach (where to look first)
- Client communication strategy
- Technical team escalation
- Short-term fixes vs. long-term solutions
- Reporting back to Account Manager
