# SEADEV/ShedPro - Project Coordinator Interview Script

**Date:** 2026-02-05
**Position:** Project Coordinator - ShedPro Team
**Interviewer:** Larry (Hiring Manager)
**Interview Duration:** 45-60 minutes
**Format:** One question at a time (Interview Protocol strict)

---

## Phase 1: Opening & Introduction (5 min)

**Q1:** Thank you for joining us today. I'm Larry, the hiring manager for this Project Coordinator role. Before we dive in, tell me - what drew you to this position at SeaDev specifically, and how does it align with what you're looking for in your next role?
*Expectation:* Understanding motivation, cultural fit, career alignment

---

## Phase 2: Background & Experience (10 min)

**Q2:** Let's start with your experience. Walk me through a recent project where you were responsible for coordinating between multiple teams and stakeholders. What was your specific role, and what was the outcome?
*Focus:* Project coordination experience, communication skills

**Q3:** SeaDev works extensively with both Vietnamese and US-based teams. Can you share an example where you've had to bridge cultural or time zone differences in a work setting? How did you ensure effective communication?
*Focus:* Cross-cultural communication, international business experience

**Q4:** The ShedPro platform serves shed builders, manufacturers, and dealers - a very specific market. Have you coordinated any projects or worked with clients in niche or specialized industries before? How did you approach learning their business model?
*Focus:* Domain knowledge adaptation, client relationship management

---

## Phase 3: Technical & Role-Specific Skills (20 min)

**Q5:** As a Project Coordinator here, you'll be managing client accounts after initial onboarding. Imagine a scenario where a ShedPro client reports that their leads from Google Ads have suddenly dropped 40%. Walk me through how you would investigate this issue and the steps you'd take to coordinate resolution between the client, our US Account Manager, and our product development team.
*Focus:* Problem-solving process, stakeholder management, technical troubleshooting

**Q6:** Our team uses various CRM systems and project management tools. What's your experience with CRM software, and can you give me an example of how you've used data from these systems to identify growth opportunities for a client or project?
*Focus:* CRM proficiency, data-driven decision making, business development mindset

**Q7:** You'll be documenting client interactions, tracking performance metrics, and preparing regular reports. Describe your approach to maintaining accurate documentation while also extracting actionable insights for both the client and our internal teams.
*Focus:* Attention to detail, analytical skills, communication excellence

**Q8:** Let's talk about the 3D visualization technology aspect. While you won't be designing the 3D models, you need to understand them well enough to train clients. Given 30 minutes to prepare, how would you approach learning the features of our 3D configurator to effectively train a new client?
*Focus:* Learning agility, technical comprehension, training approach

---

## Phase 4: Behavioral & Culture Fit (15 min)

**Q9:** One of SeaDev's core values is "Love one another well" and we have a strong focus on community transformation. Tell me about a time when you went above and beyond to help a colleague or client, even when it wasn't technically your responsibility.
*Focus:* Cultural alignment, service mindset, team collaboration

**Q10:** In product management, sometimes deadlines slip or features get delayed, affecting client promises. Describe a difficult conversation you've had with a client where their expectations couldn't be met. How did you handle it, and what was the result?
*Focus:* Client relationship management, conflict resolution, accountability

**Q11:** Our shed industry clients often have seasonal business cycles and urgent needs during peak seasons. How do you typically manage competing priorities when everything is labeled "urgent" by different clients?
*Focus:* Stress management, prioritization skills, operational excellence

---

## Phase 5: Situational Questions & Case Study (10 min)

**Q12:** **Situational Case:** A client has been using ShedPro for 6 months with consistent lead generation. They email you saying they've just discovered their competitor got featured in a major home improvement magazine, and now their leads are down 60% over the past 2 weeks. They want to know if the product isn't working anymore and expect an immediate solution. How do you respond, what steps do you take, and how do you manage their expectations?
*Focus:* Crisis management, strategic thinking, client communication under pressure

**Q13:** **Growth Opportunity:** While reviewing client metrics, you notice that dealers who set up their 3D models with premium upgrades have 3x higher average order values, but 80% of clients aren't using this feature. How would you systematically increase adoption of premium upgrades across the client base?
*Focus:* Growth mindset, client education, strategic initiatives

---

## Phase 6: Your Questions (5 min)

**Q14:** What questions do you have for me about this role, our team culture, or ShedPro's growth trajectory?
